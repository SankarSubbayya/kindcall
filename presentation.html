The problem

Caregivers see everything. The family hears nothing.

📝

Notes get lost

Daily observations live on sticky notes, texts, and memory — never aggregated into a record.

⏳

Alerts come late

A missed medication or a fall is often noticed hours later, if at all.

⌨️

Typing is the enemy

Busy, on-the-move caregivers won't stop to fill out a form.

The solution

Just talk. KindCall does the rest.

A caregiver speaks one sentence. KindCall turns rambling speech into a safe clinical record and pages the family the instant something is wrong — with no keyboard.

The differentiator

An AI that gets a senior's medication wrong is dangerous.

So every structured note is graded by an LLM-as-judge faithfulness eval through Respan: did the record actually match what the caregiver said? A low score is flagged, not trusted. This is healthcare-grade AI.
