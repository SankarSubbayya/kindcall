The differentiator

An AI that gets a senior's medication wrong is dangerous.

So every structured note is graded by an LLM-as-judge faithfulness eval through Respan — did the record actually match what the caregiver said? Low score → flagged, not trusted. That's healthcare-grade AI, not vibes.
